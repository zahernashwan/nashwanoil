About NASHWAN

35+ years of dependable industrial supply & oilfield services across Yemen and the region.

Who We Are

NASHWAN Corp. for General Trading & Oilfield Services is a trusted partner to oilfield, petrochemical, gas, drilling and industrial operators. We source and supply certified equipment from 100+ international brands, covering welding systems, industrial safety, pipes, valves & fittings, power tools and workshop solutions.

Our model is simple and reliable: clear specifications, verified procurement, careful consolidation, and door-to-door delivery supported by responsive after-sales. This is how we keep projects moving and downtime low in demanding operating environments.

What We Do

  • Strategic Sourcing: multi-brand options matched to your specs, lead time and budget.
  • Industrial Supply: welding & consumables, PPE, gas detection, pipes/valves/fittings, hand & power tools, compressors and more.
  • Project Fulfilment: kitting, labeling and documentation aligned to project BoMs.

How We Work

  1. Clarify: confirm specs, materials, sizes, ratings and documentation needs.
  2. Source: request firm quotations from qualified brands; propose value-engineered alternates when helpful.
  3. Validate: align technical datasheets and compliance statements to the request.
  4. Fulfil: place orders, inspect, kit and label per BoM; arrange shipping and customs paperwork.
  5. Support: provide delivery notes, certificates when available, and responsive after-sales.
